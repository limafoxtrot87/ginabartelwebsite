Enjoy effortless Florida living in this beautifully upgraded 2-bedroom, 2-bath villa with a den, ideally positioned on a tranquil water-view lot. Thoughtfully elevated with designer-inspired and upgrades, this move-in-ready home offers a perfect blend of comfort, functionality, and peace of mind in a low-maintenance setting.    The open-concept layout is enhanced by upgraded tile flooring throughout, elegant crown molding, and a 7.2 surround sound system, creating an inviting atmosphere for everyday living and entertaining. The kitchen and laundry areas feature custom cabinetry, cabinet organizers, and a stackable washer and dryer, maximizing storage and efficiency.    The spacious primary suite provides a serene retreat, while the split-bedroom design ensures privacy for guests. A versatile den offers flexible space ideal for a home office, library, or additional sitting area. Additional interior features include plantation shutters, upgraded ceiling fans, a Culligan water softener, whole-house gutters, and a fully operational security system.    Outdoor living is equally impressive with a screened front porch, an expanded paver patio, professional landscaping, a screened garage, and a screened lanai overlooking peaceful water views—perfect for morning coffee or unwinding at sunset. For added security and storm protection, new impact glass has been installed on all windows and rear sliding doors, providing comfort and confidence year-round.    Residents of this vibrant community enjoy an exceptional array of amenities designed to support an active, low-maintenance lifestyle. The clubhouse features a bistro, fitness center, beach-entry pool, pickleball and tennis courts, bocce ball, game rooms, and organized social activities. Pet lovers will appreciate two conveniently located dog parks near the clubhouse.        Ideally located just minutes from Gulf Coast beaches, premier shopping and dining, and Tampa International Airport, this home offers the perfect balance of relaxation, recreation, and convenience.
